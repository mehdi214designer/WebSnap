Test 9 — text truncation (ellipsis + line-clamp — expected: text overflows in Figma)
white-space: nowrap + ellipsis
This is a very long sentence that gets cut off with an ellipsis because it overflows its single-line container box
Browser clips at container edge. Importer captures full text — no truncation in Figma.
-webkit-line-clamp: 3 lines
This paragraph has too much content to show in three lines. The browser clips it gracefully with an ellipsis at the end. But the captured text is the full string, so in Figma it wraps to its natural height. Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipiscing elit.
Expected: text runs longer in Figma than on screen.
-webkit-line-clamp: 2 lines
A two-line clamp cuts off everything after the second line. This trailing text is invisible in the browser but will render fully in Figma since we capture the full textContent, not the clamped display.
Expected: text overflows card bottom in Figma.
